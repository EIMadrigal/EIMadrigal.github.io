## Motivation
$f(\mathbf{x}) = \boldsymbol{\beta}^\top\mathbf{x}$
$$\frac{df}{d\mathbf{x}} = \begin{bmatrix}
\frac{df}{dx_1} \\
\vdots \\
\frac{df}{dx_n}
\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}
\beta_1 \\
\vdots \\
\beta_n
\end{bmatrix} = \boldsymbol{\beta}$$

$\frac{d}{d\mathbf{x}}(\mathbf{x}^\top A \mathbf{x}) = (\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{A}^\top)\mathbf{x}.$
